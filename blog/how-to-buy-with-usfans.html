Typical tracking timeline (E-EMS to Spain and Europe):

  • Day 14-18: "Out for delivery" — On the truck heading to your address.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Even experienced buyers make these mistakes. Learn from them to save time, money and frustration.

  • Ignoring volumetric weight calculations. New buyers see "1.2 kg actual weight" and budget $12 for shipping, only to find the billable weight is 2.6 kg due to box dimensions. Always check USFans shipping calculator before confirming your order. The volumetric weight formula is: (L×W×H cm) ÷ 5,000.
  • Not reviewing QC photos thoroughly. Rushing through QC approval because you're excited to receive your items is the fastest path to disappointment. Spend 10 minutes per item. Zoom into every detail. If you spot a defect, request a swap — it's free at the warehouse stage.
  • Ordering without checking the size chart. Assuming "I'm a size Large" without verifying the seller's specific measurements leads to ill-fitting clothes. Chinese brands vary enormously. One seller's XL is another's M. Always use the provided size chart in cm.
  • Forgetting to enter the referral code. If you register without code RCGD5Y, you permanently lose the €800 welcome bonus. The code cannot be applied retroactively after your first order has shipped. Enter it during registration or in your profile before placing any orders.
  • Shipping a single item. The first 500g of any package costs the most. A single 800g item might cost $18 to ship, while three items totaling 2.5kg might cost $28 total. The shipping cost per item drops dramatically with consolidation.
  • Using DHL Express for low-value shipments. Unless your items are urgent or worth over $200, DHL is overkill. E-EMS delivers reliably in 12-18 days at 60% less cost. Save DHL for special situations.
  • Not accounting for customs duties. USFans shipping does not include import taxes. For Spain and Europe, most packages under €150 may pass without duty. For the UK and EU, expect to pay VAT (20% in UK, ~19-25% in EU countries) on packages exceeding the de minimis threshold. Budget an extra 20-25% if you're in Europe.
