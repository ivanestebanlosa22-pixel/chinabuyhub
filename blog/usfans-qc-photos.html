USFans QC Photo Guide: Master Quality Control

Quality Control photos are your most powerful tool as a buyer. They're your only chance to inspect products before they ship internationally. Here we explain exactly what to look for, what USFans provides and how to spot problems before approving.

What QC Photos Does USFans Provide?

Each order at USFans includes between 3 and 5 free QC photos. These are taken by USFans warehouse staff when your product arrives from the seller. Here's what the standard package includes:

  • Front view — Full product photo from the front, showing overall shape and appearance
  • Back view — Complete back of the item, including heel details for shoes
  • Logo/brand close-up — Macro photo of the logo, verifying branding accuracy
  • Size tag — Confirmation that the correct size was shipped
  • Accessories photo — All included items (extra laces, tags, box, dust bags)

QC Photos Are Usually Ready in 24 Hours

Standard processing time is 24-48 hours after the product arrives at the warehouse. During peak shopping seasons (11/11, 12/12, Chinese New Year), it can take up to 3 days. You'll receive a notification in your USFans dashboard when photos are ready.

How to Review QC Photos: Complete Checklist

SKU and Order Match

  • ✓ The product model matches what you ordered (color, style, version)
  • ✓ The size tag shows the correct size you selected
  • ✓ The seller name on the package matches your order

Visual Quality — Sneakers

  • ✓ The Swoosh/logo shape, placement and angle are correct
  • ✓ Stitching is straight and even — no loose threads
  • ✓ Toe box shape matches the original (not too square or flat)
  • ✓ Tongue tag text and placement are accurate
  • ✓ Sole color and pattern match the expected batch
  • ✓ Heel height and shape look correct
  • ✓ No visible glue stains around the sole or midsole
  • ✓ Both shoes appear to be the same size and shape

Visual Quality — Clothing and Hoodies

  • ✓ The print/embroidery is centered and not crooked
  • ✓ Fabric color matches the listing photos (lighting can affect this)
  • ✓ Neck and wash labels are present and look correct
  • ✓ No visible stains, wrinkles or fabric defects
  • ✓ Zippers and buttons work and are not damaged

Measurements and Fit

  • ✓ Insole measurement matches your expected size (for shoes)
  • ✓ Chest/length measurements match the size chart (for clothing)
  • ✓ Request measurement photos if not included in standard QC

How to Request Additional QC Photos

Standard QC photos cover the basics, but sometimes you need a more detailed inspection of specific aspects. USFans lets you request additional HD photos for $0.50 each. Here's how:

  1. Log into your USFans account and go to "Orders" → "In Warehouse"
  2. Click on the order to view standard QC photos
  3. Click "Request More Photos" or contact support via chat
  4. Specify exactly what you want photographed:
    • "Close-up of size tag with natural light"
    • "3M reflective test photo with flash"
    • "Insole measurement with ruler"
    • "Side-by-side comparison of left and right shoe"
    • "Interior stitching detail"
  5. Additional photos are delivered within 24 hours

Pro Tip: Always Request These 3 Additional Photos

1) Insole measurement with ruler (confirms fit), 2) Photo with natural light (warehouse LED lights can distort colors), 3) Flash photo for 3M/reflective materials. These 3 photos cost $1.50 total and can prevent costly returns.

Red Flags: When to Reject or Exchange

Reasons for Immediate Exchange/Return

1) Wrong size or model sent, 2) Visible defects (stains, tears, missing parts), 3) Logo noticeably off-center or poorly printed, 4) Color is completely different from the listing, 5) Product looks used or damaged. In these cases, click "Exchange" or "Return" immediately — it's free before international shipping.

What Happens After QC Approval?

Once you click "Approve" on your QC photos, USFans begins preparing your package for international shipping. At this point, returns and exchanges become difficult and costly. That's why carefully reviewing QC photos is the most important step of the entire purchase process.

You Cannot Cancel After Approval

Once QC is approved, the item is considered accepted. You cannot change your mind or request a different product. If a defect is discovered after shipping, you may be eligible for a partial refund or discount on your next order — but full refunds are rare after international dispatch. Spend 5-10 minutes reviewing each QC photo. It's worth it.

Frequently Asked Questions about USFans QC Photos

Are QC photos really free at USFans?

Yes, between 3 and 5 standard QC photos are completely free on every order. Additional close-up HD photos and measurement photos cost $0.50 each. This is better than many agents that charge $1-3 for basic QC.

How long do QC photos take?

Typically 24 hours after arriving at the warehouse. During peak season (November-December), up to 48 hours. The seller-to-warehouse shipping time (2-5 days) is separate from QC photography time.

What if QC photos show a different product than what I ordered?

Click "Exchange" or "Return" and explain the issue in the notes. USFans will contact the seller for a replacement. This is free as long as the product hasn't left the warehouse. Provide clear screenshots and descriptions of what's wrong.

Can I compare QC photos with original product photos?

Yes, and you should. Have photos of the original/authentic product ready when reviewing QC. Compare side by side: logo placement, color tone, material texture, shape. Online communities like r/fashionreps often have reference photos of the original product you can use for comparison.

How do I know if measurements are correct in QC photos?

Always request a measurement photo of the insole with a ruler for shoes. For clothing, request chest width, shoulder width and length measurements. USFans staff will place a measuring tape directly on the item and photograph it ($0.50 extra per measurement).
