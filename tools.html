Smart Buyer's Toolkit

Professional Tools for Shopping with Chinese Agents

A curated set of free tools designed for premium product buyers, resellers and collectors who use Chinese agents. Estimate shipping costs, convert product links, understand sizing, plan for customs and even find products using AI image recognition.

ChinaBuyHub Tools is a premium utility suite built specifically for buyers using Chinese shopping agents to order premium and fashion products. Instead of generic calculators, each tool is tailored for real agent workflows: shipping cost patterns, consolidation strategies, customs thresholds and multi-agent link conversion.

Our goal is to help you make safer, smarter and more cost-effective decisions on every order while keeping your agent's affiliate structure completely private on the backend. All examples are estimates and should always be verified with your preferred agent before paying.
