What is Taobao?

Taobao (??) is China's largest online marketplace, owned and operated by Alibaba Group. Additional Cost Factors

  • Volumetric Weight: Carriers charge by whichever is higher: actual weight or volumetric weight (length × width × height ÷ 5000). Bulky lightweight items like cushions or jackets may cost more than expected.

Common Taobao Mistakes to Avoid

Learning from others' mistakes can save you time, money, and frustration. These are the most common errors international buyers encounter when buying on Taobao:

❌ Mistake 1: Not Using Quality Control Photos

Some buyers rush to ship their items without carefully reviewing QC photos. Always inspect each product before it leaves the warehouse. QC photos are your only chance to catch defects, wrong sizes, or incorrect items before international shipping makes returns nearly impossible.

❌ Mistake 2: Ignoring Size Charts

Assuming your usual size will fit is the #1 cause of disappointing Taobao purchases. Always measure yourself and compare with the seller's specific size chart. Chinese sizes are smaller than Western sizes, especially in width and shoulders.

❌ Mistake 3: Buying Without Reviews

New listings with no purchase history are risky. Focus on products with high sales volumes and positive reviews. Customer review photos are your best indicator of actual product quality.

❌ Mistake 4: Shipping Items Individually

Shipping each item as it arrives at the warehouse is expensive. Use the free storage period (30-90 days) to consolidate multiple items into one package and save 40-60% on shipping costs.

❌ Mistake 5: Not Checking Exchange Rates

Agent prices are shown in CNY (Chinese Yuan). Exchange rates fluctuate, and your bank or payment provider may charge foreign transaction fees. Use a card with no foreign transaction fees and pay attention to the exchange rate your agent uses.

❌ Mistake 6: Choosing the Cheapest Agent

Agents with low fees may seem attractive, but they often cut costs on QC photos, customer service, and shipping quality. A few extra euros in service fees are worth it for reliable buyer protection and responsive support when things go wrong.
