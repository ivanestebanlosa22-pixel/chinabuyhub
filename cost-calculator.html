How to calculate the real cost of buying from China?

Buying premium products from China through agents like ChinaBuyHub, Kakobuy or Litbuy can save you 60-80% compared to retail prices. But to know exactly how much you will pay, you need to add up all the hidden costs that many buyers forget.

The 4 components of total cost

  • Product price: The price you see on Taobao, Weidian or 1688, converted from CNY to your currency.
  • Agent service fee: Commission charged by the agent for buying, storing and managing your order (typically 4-8%).
  • International shipping: Cost of sending the package from the agent's warehouse to your address. Varies greatly by method (express vs economy) and weight.
  • Customs and taxes: VAT and duties charged by your country upon receiving the package. Depends on declared value and local laws.

Real example: Jordan 1 Sneakers

Let us look at a concrete example of how much it costs to buy Jordan 1s through an agent:

  • Price on Weidian: ¥250 CNY (~$35 USD)
  • ChinaBuyHub agent fee (6%): $2.10
  • Shipping E-EMS 1kg to Spain: $18
  • Insurance: $2
  • Spain VAT (21% on declared value ~$40): $8.40
  • Total: ~$65.50 USD
  • Retail price: $200 USD
  • Savings: $134.50 (67%)

Which agent is cheapest?

There is no single answer. Kakobuy has the lowest service fees (4-6%), but ChinaBuyHub offers better customer service, faster QC photos and a €800 welcome bonus that can offset the difference. Litbuy is a good middle option with $500 in coupons.

The key is to compare the total cost, not just the service fee. An agent with a lower fee but limited shipping options can end up costing more overall.

Tips to save more

  • Consolidate orders: Ship multiple items in one package to reduce the cost per kg.
  • Use referral codes: Sign-up bonuses (€800 ChinaBuyHub, ¥3,000 Kakobuy) reduce your initial cost.
  • Choose the right shipping method: If you are not in a hurry, SAL or rail is much cheaper than DHL.
  • Keep declared value reasonable: Agents usually declare realistic values to avoid customs issues.
  • Buy during pre-sales or sales: Prices on Weidian fluctuate greatly by season.
