How Referral Codes Work

Referral codes are a mutual benefit system used by shopping agents to attract new customers. Here are the details behind the system:

For you (the buyer): When you register a new account using a referral code or referral link, the agent credits your account with a welcome bonus. This bonus typically comes in the form of coupons, balance credits, or automatic fee reductions that apply on your first orders. You don't need to do anything extra ? the bonus applies automatically based on your order value.

For the referrer: The person who shared the code receives a small commission or credit when you sign up and make your first purchase. This incentivizes content creators, reviewers, and communities to share active codes with new buyers.

Key rules to understand:

  • New accounts only ? You cannot apply a referral code to an existing account. If you already have an account with an agent, that code opportunity has passed.
  • One code per agent ? Each platform allows only one referral code per registration. Choose the best code before signing up.
  • No stacking ? Referral bonuses cannot be combined with other promotional codes or special offers on the same order.
  • Bonus expiry ? Individual coupon credits within your bonus package typically expire after 30-90 days. Use them promptly.
  • Minimum orders may apply ? Some bonus tiers only unlock after reaching a minimum order value (e.g., ?50+ for the first coupon tier on ChinaBuyHub).

?? Important Note

Referral codes must be entered during registration ? you cannot add them after creating your account. If you register without a code, you permanently lose the welcome bonus. Take 10 seconds to use a referral link before creating your account.
