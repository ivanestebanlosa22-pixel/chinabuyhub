Complete Guide: How to Buy with Chinese Agents

Buying products directly from China through shopping agents allows you to access high-quality products at incredible prices. This guide will explain step by step how the process works, what to expect and how to avoid common problems.

What is a Shopping Agent?

A Chinese shopping agent acts as an intermediary between you and sellers on Chinese platforms like Weidian, Taobao and 1688. The agent:

  • Purchases products on your behalf using their local account
  • Inspects quality and sends you verification photos (QC Photos)
  • Consolidates multiple products into one package
  • Manages international shipping to your address
  • Provides customer support in English/Spanish

Main Advantage

Agents eliminate the language barrier, facilitate international payments and ensure you receive exactly what you ordered before shipping it to your country.

Select size, color and quantity. Then proceed to payment:

  • Payment methods: PayPal, credit/debit card, bank transfer, cryptocurrencies
  • Initial cost: You only pay the product price + agent service fee (typically 5-10%)
  • International shipping: Calculated later, once your products are consolidated
4
The Agent Buys the Product

The agent places the order with the Chinese seller. This process typically takes:

  • In-stock products: 2-5 business days
  • Made-to-order products: 7-15 business days
  • Peak season (11/11, Black Friday): Up to 30 days

You can track your order status in real-time from your agent account.

5
Quality Control Photos (QC)

This is the most important step! When the product arrives at the agent's warehouse, they send you detailed photos (QC Photos) so you can verify:

  • That it's the correct product (model, color, size)
  • That there are no visible defects or damage
  • That stitching, logos and details are well finished
  • That it matches the original listing photos

Pro Tip: Request Detailed Photos

Most agents offer free basic QC photos. If you want more detailed photos of specific areas (e.g., a t-shirt logo), you can request additional HD photos for a small fee ($0.50-1 per photo).

6
Approve or Reject the Product

After reviewing the QC photos, you have three options:

  1. APPROVE: The product is perfect → Proceed to shipping
  2. EXCHANGE: There's a minor defect → Request a swap for another unit
  3. RETURN: Doesn't meet expectations → Return and request a refund

Important About Returns

Exchanges and returns are free while the product is in the agent's warehouse. Once shipped internationally, returns are very expensive and complicated. Review QC photos carefully before approving!

7
Consolidate and Calculate Shipping

If you bought multiple products, the agent consolidates them into one package to save on shipping costs. The shipping cost depends on:

  • Volumetric weight: Calculated based on package weight and dimensions
  • Destination: Country and delivery region
  • Shipping method: Economy (15-30 days) vs Express (5-10 days)
  • Declared value: Affects insurance and potential customs duties

Once shipping is calculated, you pay the final cost. The agent packs your products securely and ships the package. You will receive:

Pro Tips to Save Money

1. Bundle Multiple Purchases

International shipping is more efficient when you consolidate multiple products into one package. Wait until you have several items before shipping.

2. Remove Boxes and Unnecessary Packaging

Shoeboxes, brand bags and bulky packaging increase weight and shipping cost. Ask the agent to remove them if you don't need them (reduces weight by up to 30%).

3. Declare a Lower Value (With Caution)

Agents can declare a lower value than the real one to reduce customs taxes. Typically declared between $10-15 per package. Note: This reduces insurance in case of loss.

4. Use Slow Shipping for Heavy Loads

For packages of 5kg or more, sea freight can save up to 70% of the cost compared to air shipping. Ideal if you're not in a hurry.

5. Take Advantage of Seasonal Promotions

During 11/11 (Singles' Day) and 12/12, many sellers offer 20-40% discounts. Agents also often have free shipping promotions or reduced rates.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

⚠ Not Reviewing QC Photos Carefully

This is your only opportunity to verify the product before international shipping. Spend 5-10 minutes reviewing every detail. If something doesn't look right, request a swap or return.

⚠ Not Confirming the Correct Size

Chinese sizes are generally smaller than European. Always consult the seller's size chart and when in doubt, order one size up.

⚠ Expecting Identical Quality to Original

Although many high-quality alternatives have very good quality, they are not original products. They may have small differences in materials, stitching or finishes. Read reviews from other buyers before ordering.

⚠ Not Considering Delivery Times

The complete process (purchase → QC → shipping → delivery) can take between 15 and 45 days. Don't buy products for last-minute events. Plan ahead.

⚠ Ignoring Your Country's Customs Regulations

Each country has limits on what can be imported and the maximum value without taxes. Research your country's regulations before making large purchases.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is It Safe to Buy with Chinese Agents?

Yes, when you use established and trusted agents like the ones we recommend (USFans, Litbuy, Kakobuy). These agents process thousands of daily orders and have buyer protection systems. Always use the agent's official payment methods.

What Happens if My Package is Lost or Held at Customs?

Agents offer shipping insurance (optional, ~3% of declared value). If your package is lost, the insurance reimburses you. For customs holds, the agent provides the necessary documentation for clearance. In rare cases, you may have to pay local taxes.

How Much Does a Complete Purchase Really Cost?

Real example (sneakers at $50):

  • Product: $50
  • Agent commission (10%): $5
  • Shipping (0.8kg): $12
  • Total: $67

Compared to over $200 for the original product, you still save significantly.

Can I Return Products After Receiving Them?

International returns are very expensive (often more than the product). That's why it's CRITICAL to review QC photos carefully before approving shipping. Some agents offer free returns before international shipping.

Do Products Have a Warranty?

Premium version products generally do not have a formal warranty. However, if you receive a defective product that doesn't match the approved QC photos, the agent typically offers solutions (partial refund, discount on next purchase).
