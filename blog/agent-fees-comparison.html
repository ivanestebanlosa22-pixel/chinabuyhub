Understanding Shopping Agent Fees

Shopping agents act as intermediaries between international buyers and Chinese sellers. They handle the entire purchasing process: placing orders on your behalf, receiving packages at their Chinese warehouse, performing quality control, repackaging items and shipping them internationally to your door. For these services, agents charge various fees.

What Service Fees Cover

The main fee charged by shopping agents is the service fee — typically a percentage of the product cost. This fee covers:

  • Purchasing assistance: Placing orders on Chinese platforms, communicating with sellers and handling transactions on your behalf.
  • Quality control (QC) photos: Taking detailed photos of your items before shipping so you can inspect quality, verify defects and confirm correctness.
  • Warehouse storage: Receiving and storing your packages until you are ready to ship them internationally.
  • Repackaging: Consolidating multiple packages, adding protective packaging and removing unnecessary boxes to reduce shipping costs.
  • Customer support: Assisting with order issues, returns, refunds and providing guidance throughout the purchasing process.

Beyond the service fee, there are several other costs to consider. Payment processing fees cover the cost of converting your local currency to Chinese Yuan and processing international payments. Domestic shipping is the cost of shipping within China from the seller to the agent's warehouse. International shipping is often the largest additional cost, varying by weight, dimensions, destination and shipping method. Additional fees may include storage charges for prolonged warehouse storage, insurance for high-value items, special handling for fragile items and express shipping options for guaranteed delivery.

Hidden Fees to Watch For

Even with transparent pricing from major agents, there are several additional costs that can surprise buyers. Here are the most common hidden fees and how to avoid them:

Extended Storage Fees

Most agents offer free storage for a limited period (30-90 days), but start charging once that period expires. If you forget packages in your warehouse, you could accumulate significant daily storage charges. Tip: Keep track of your storage deadlines and ship items promptly. USFans offers the longest free storage at 90 days, giving you more flexibility.

Currency Conversion Margins

Some agents add a hidden 0.5-3% margin on top of the market exchange rate when converting your currency to Chinese Yuan. This fee is not always clearly disclosed upfront. Tip: Compare the exchange rate offered by the agent with the current market rate. USFans and Litbuy include conversion costs in their payment processing fee, while Kakobuy may charge a separate conversion fee.

Oversized Package Fees

Packages that exceed standard size or weight limits may incur additional handling charges. Items like furniture, large electronics or bulky clothing can trigger these surcharges. Tip: Check the agent's size and weight limits before ordering. Ask for package dimensions in your QC photos to avoid surprises.

Special Handling Fees

Fragile items, liquids, batteries and other restricted items may require special packaging or shipping methods that incur additional fees. Tip: Always declare special item types upfront and ask about any additional charges before placing your order.

Insurance Costs

Although insurance is optional, some agents automatically include it for high-value items. Insurance typically costs 1-5% of the declared value. Tip: Review your order carefully before confirming to ensure you are not being charged for unwanted insurance. Consider whether you actually need insurance for your specific purchase.

Return and Cancellation Fees

If you need to return an item or cancel an order, some agents charge a fee for managing the return process. The service fee may or may not be refunded depending on the agent's policy. Tip: Review the agent's return policy before ordering. USFans offers the most generous refund policy, with the full service fee refunded on returns.
