Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use multiple shopping agents at the same time?
Yes. Many experienced buyers use different agents for different purposes. For example, use USFans for large orders to Europe and Kakobuy for quick single-item orders. There are no restrictions on how many agents you can sign up for.
Which agent has the cheapest shipping?
Shipping costs are comparable across the three agents, with differences of 2-5€ per kg depending on the specific shipping line. USFans offers more shipping line options. Litbuy tends to be slightly cheaper on economy lines. Kakobuy is competitive on DHL rates.
Do I have to pay to use these shopping agents?
Registration is free on all three platforms. You only pay when you buy items (item cost + service fee + shipping). Welcome bonuses with codes RCGD5Y, FINDSES and YBMHFG55L help offset initial costs. There are no subscription or membership fees.
